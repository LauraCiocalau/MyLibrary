About My Library App

My Library is an easy-to-use web application that helps you manage your personal library of books. Whether you want to keep track of what you’ve read, plan your next read, or organize your collection, MyLibrary has the tools to make it simple and enjoyable. Below is an overview of its key features:

User Account Management

Sign Up:

You can quickly create an account without needing to confirm your email, making it a hassle-free experience.

Login:

Once you’ve signed up, you can log in securely, and the system remembers you throughout your session until you log out.

Delete Account:

If you no longer wish to use My Library, you can easily delete your account with just a few clicks.

Book Management

Add Books:

You can add new books to your library, including details like the title, author, genre, and year of publication. You can also upload a cover image for your books.

View Books:

See your entire book collection in one place, customized just for you.

Advanced Search:

The search functionality allows users to quickly find the desired books based on several criteria:

Search by Title:

Enter the full or partial title to find books quickly.

Search by Author:

Look for books by a specific author using either their full name or just a part of it.

Search by Year:

Filter books based on the year of publication.

Search by Category:

Select a literary genre (e.g., Fantasy, Mystery, Romance, etc.) to view all books in that category.

Search by Status:

Allows filtering of books based on their status (Read, Unread, Reading).

Statistics:

Displays the total number of books in the collection and the number of books that meet the search criteria.

Update Books:

If you make a mistake or want to update the information about a book, you can easily modify the book’s details.

Delete Books:

Want to remove a book from your collection? You can delete it whenever you want.

User Profile Management

Profile Information:

You can view your account details, like your username and email address, and manage your information easily.

Security Features

Safe and Secure:

My Library takes your security seriously, using advanced tools to keep your data safe and ensure you can access your account securely.

Activity Tracking:

The app keeps logs of user activities to make sure everything is running smoothly and safely.

Key Pages of My Library

Landing Page:

This is the first page you see when you visit My Library. Here, you can log in or sign up if you’re a new user.

Forgot Password Page:

If you forget your password, you can request a password reset link to be sent to your email.

Reset Password Page:

After receiving the reset link in your email, you can use this page to create a new password.

Edit Book Page:

This page lets you update details about the books you’ve already added, like changing the cover image or correcting the author’s name.

How Different Parts of the Application Work Together

Account Management:

The landing page lets you log in and sign up, connecting you to your library and profile.

Password Reset:

If you forget your password, the app makes it easy to reset it by sending you a link through email.

Book Editing:

When you need to update information about a book, you can easily edit the details using the Edit Book page.

Technology Behind the Scenes

Backend (the engine behind the app):

MyLibrary is powered by .NET, a reliable technology that ensures everything runs smoothly and securely.

User Security:

My Library uses trusted methods to make sure your account is safe.

Database:

All your book and account details are safely stored in a database, ensuring you can access them anytime.

In short, My Library is a comprehensive platform for book lovers to easily manage their personal libraries without needing to worry about complex technical details.
